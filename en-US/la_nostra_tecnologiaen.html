Our technology

 
FIRST AND ONLY SYSTEM FOR THE CONSTRUCTION OF CLIMBING WALLS AVAILABLE IN ASSEMBLY KIT-BOX.

The panels in P.E.T offered by Climblock are an absolute novelty, unique on the market, are meant as a highly technological tool and at the same time very handy. They have also been conceived in a wholly recycled material (the P.E.T. reuse), in a complete environment-friendly compliance with requirements. They have been designed in collaboration with the best national experts in the field of plastics.
Considering, as well, the continuous research and innovation in the field, the use of such materials in the construction of our product makes a constant improvement of it possible.
The assembly system of modules, measuring 40x40 cm, may operate in different ways, closely suiting customer needs and technical characteristics of the wall which is meant to be built:
this technically most advanced system may build considerably high walls with remarkable climbing possibilities (also thanks to the light and versatile material); it combines the reliable traditional ‘dalmine’ bar and clamp system with a set of stanchions consisting in square bars to which the panels are fastened. The whole structure is resting onto a self-supporting base or against a building. The system implies field expertise in the planning and realization of the structure, but gives excellent results with a variety of possibilities and solutions.
The easiest system consists of panels directly applied on wall by self-threading screws onto wooden laths previously fixed to the building. It implements small do-it-yourself walls of simple assembly and minimal costs.
