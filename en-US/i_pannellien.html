The panels

 
THE CLIMBLOCK PANEL

Panels are available in a great range of colours, thus achieving a modern and attractive look, suitable to any place and situation. They are devised in two versions: with plane surface or such as to accept dovetailed hand-grips.
Besides, panels could be concave or convex, for the implementation of accentuated curves. As counter-effect to the rotation of grips two solutions are proposed: one through a special coating of the support surface of grips on panel, that reduces, in the case of grips going to pieces the fall of fragments on to the ground as well, a second one by applying a little blockage tooth.
Also ‘micro’ type grips can be applied with self-threading screws. Panels can be cut and shaped as one wishes for patching up or geometrical volumes.
The density of piercing requires 100 axle boxes per square meter; axle boxes are merged into plastic and enable a quick and simple clasping of grips.
The resistance of the material to climatic agents, thanks to the anti UV treatment of the materials used, and the remarkably simple substitution of modules reduces maintenance costs. The panel is guaranteed by producer for two years for outdoor use and for five years for indoor use.
The firm has set up an installation to test the product against climatic agents and for five years the wall has been exposed to the weather without physical or mechanical alteration of the material.
Under request the panel could be produced and certified in class one reaction to fire, according to Italian regulations.
